Paris

I visited Paris for the first time when I was about 5 years old. It took a sixteen-hour-long train ride to arrive to france from Italy. Paris i best known for the Eiffel Tower and its rich fashion influence. Paris is one of the few world capitals that has rarely seen destruction by catastrophe or war. For this, even its earliest history is still visible in its streetmap, and centuries of rulers adding their respective architectural marks on the capital has resulted in an accumulated wealth of history-rich monuments and buildings whose beauty played a large part in giving the city the reputation it has today.
